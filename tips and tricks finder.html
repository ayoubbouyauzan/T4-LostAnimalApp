I found a pet, what do I do

Stay calm and follow these following steps:
Go to your account and click the ‘missing’ button on your navigation bar. Scroll through the list to see if the pet is there or not. If the pet is not there and it seems friendly enough to touch, walk up to the pet and scan its QR code on their collar. If it also doesn’t show up as lost and you are 100% sure the pet is lost; you can create a profile for this lost pet inside the app.

This pet looks ill, what do I do?

If you think the pet is ill, please contact your nearest veterinarian. Breda contains lots of veterinarians. The one most close to the Centre is Dierenkliniek Breda. It is best to call before departure. Their phone number is 076-5600666

Where can I bring a pet to if I find it?

If you found a pet and don’t have the time to wait for the owner after you contacted them, bring the pet to the nearest shelter; Dierenbescherming Breda (See local shelters for their contact details).

How do I know if a pet is lost?

It is hard to tell if an animal is lost since it is natural for pets to ‘wander’ sometimes. Or for dogs to run off from their owner for a few blocks and then return.
Luckily with this app you can check if the pet is lost. By entering the list of lost pets in the area. If the animal is not on the list, you can scan the QR code attached to the collar of the animal. It would show up there if the owner reported the pet missing or not. If it doesn’t, the pet has not been reported lost yet so you can keep walking.

I am scared to approach the pet, what do I do?

If you are scared to approach a pet it is in your best interest to stay as calm as possible. Look up the list of lost pets in the area you are in and see if the pet is listed in there. If not, and you think the pet is truly lost contact the animal shelter for help with catching the pet and bringing him in. From there you can safely look up the owner of the pet (see local shelters for their contact details).
