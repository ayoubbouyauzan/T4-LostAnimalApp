About this app

This app has been established because of a school project organized by Breda University of Applied Sciences. The lecturers assisting the team behind this app are Rob Verhagen, Laura Pardo, and Raphael Velt. They contributed by answering our questions, creating the client brief and checking up if everything was going correctly. Our team consists of nine people which is sub divided into different teams.

Together we created this app for you to help you when your pet is lost or when you have found a pet. We created it so that not everyone needs to have the app. This makes it easier and more practical. We hope this app has helped you found your pet or made you return a pet to its rightful owner.

Thank you for using Paw Tracker!

Sincerely,
The Paw Tracker Team.
